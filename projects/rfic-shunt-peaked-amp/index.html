CMOS Shunt-Peaked Amplifier Verification

Cadence/Spectre • AC analysis • LD sweep • gain/BW extraction • PVT robustness

Project Summary

In this project, I designed and verified a CMOS shunt-peaked amplifier to meet target gain, bandwidth, and power dissipation requirements. I used an LD sweep to tune the shunt-peaking behavior and ran PVT corner simulations to evaluate robustness. Across corners, the amplifier maintained the required frequency-domain behavior while meeting power constraints.

  • Spec-driven tuning: Swept the shunt-peaking inductor (LD) to hit the target peaking and bandwidth.
  • Automated measurements: Extracted gain at specific frequencies, 3-dB bandwidth, and power using ADE expressions.
  • Corner verification: Compared gain/BW variation across PVT while keeping power within limits.
  • Debugging workflow: Resolved “waveform vs point” evaluation issues and validated calculator outputs.
