2.4-GHz Cascode CMOS LNA Design (SpectreRF)

Cadence SpectreRF • S-parameters • NF • matching • P1dB/IIP3

Project Summary

This project documents my end-to-end RFIC verification flow for a 2.4–2.48 GHz cascode CMOS LNA in Cadence/SpectreRF. I focused on meeting a spec-driven target set by tuning input/output matching, validating S-parameters and noise figure, and checking power and linearity constraints.

  • Matching & gain: Iterated matching to meet band performance and return-loss goals.
  • Noise: Verified NF across the band and tracked trade-offs vs. gain/matching.
  • Power & linearity: Monitored PDC from bias and evaluated linearity metrics (P1dB/IIP3).
